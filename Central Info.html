"Barangay Central"

Formerly Poblacion,is a barangay in the municipality of Arteche, in the province of Eastern Samar. It's population as determined by the 2020 Census was 377. This represented 2.30% of the total population of Arteche.


Historical population

The population of Central fell from 560 in 1990 to 377 in 2020, an decrease of 183 people over the course of 30 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a positive growth rate of 2.00%, or an decrease of 38 people, from the previous population of 415 in 2015.
