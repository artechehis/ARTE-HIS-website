"Hon. Vicente S. Ocfemia"

Subsequently, on February 12, 1990, then Municipal Vice-Mayor Vicente S. Ocfemia assumed the office of the municipal Mayor. It was during this time that strong winds and unfavorable weather brought Taiwanese fishermen to the shores of Arteche. The Taiwanese was grateful for the hospitality of the Artechehanons that they built the Taiwanese Memorial Arch as a symbol of thanks and friendship with the Artechehanons.The Taiwanese who survived from the shipwrecked went back home and brought their dead brothers with them.
