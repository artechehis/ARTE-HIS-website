"Barangay Inayawan"

Is a barangay in the municipality of Arteche, in the province of Eastern Samar. It's population as determined by the 2020 Census was 175. This represented 1.07% of the total population of Arteche.


Historical population

The population of Inayawan grew from 65 in 1990 to 175 in 2020, an increase of 110 people over the course of 30 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a negative growth rate of 4.52%, or an deccrease of 43 people, from the previous population of 218 in 2015.
