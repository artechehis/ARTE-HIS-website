"Barangay Bigo"

Is a barangay in the municipality of Arteche, in the province of Eastern Samar. It's population as determined by the 2020 Census was 388. This represented 2.37% of the total population of Arteche.


Historical population

The population of Bigo grew from 231 in 1990 to 388 in 2020, an increase of 157 people over the course of 30 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a positive growth rate of 3.14%, or an increase of 53 people, from the previous population of 335 in 2015.
