"Barangay Tangbo"

Is a barangay in the municipality of Arteche, in the province of Eastern Samar. It's population as determined by the 2020 Census was 1,444. This represented 8.83% of the total population of Arteche.


Historical population

The population of Tangbo grew from 945 in 1990 to 1444 in 2020, an increase of 499 people over the course of 30 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a positive growth rate of 0.76%, or an increase of 51 people, from the previous population of 1,393 in 2015.
