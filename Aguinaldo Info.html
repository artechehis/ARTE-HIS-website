"Barangay Aguinaldo"

Is a barangay in the municipality of Arteche, in the province of Eastern Samar. It's population as determined by the 2020 Census was 244. This represented 1.49% of the total population of Arteche.


Historical population

The population of Aguinaldo grew from 139 in 1990 to 244 in 2020, an increase of 105 people over the course of 30 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a positive growth rate of 7.51%, or an increase of 71 people, from the previous population of 173 in 2015.
