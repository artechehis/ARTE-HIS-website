"Barangay Buenavista"

Is a barangay in the municipality of Arteche, in the province of Eastern Samar. It's population as determined by the 2020 Census was 455. This represented 2.78% of the total population of Arteche.


Historical population

The population of Buenavista grew from 206 in 1990 to 455 in 2020, an increase of 249 people over the course of 30 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a positive growth rate of 0.37%, or a increase of 8 people, from the previous population of 463 in 2015.
